At the heart of our ecosystem lies the $CHAOS token, our governance and rewards token designed to drive platform engagement and innovation. $CHAOS aligns platform growth with user prosperity through:

- **Governance Rights**: Active participation in CHAOS FINANCE’s future direction and decision-making.
- **Sonic Participation**: Control over sSONIC accumulated by CHAOS FINANCE, granting voting rights in Sonic’s key decisions.
- **Participation Incentives**: Rewards for active participation when holding locked CHAOS.
- **Buy Back and Token Burn**: Through staking reward fees, MEV profits, and more.
- **Voting Incentives**: Benefits from CHAOS FINANCE partners seeking governance votes.
